# Privacy Policy

This application is a private, internal automation tool used by Rego Design LLC to assist with sales order processing.

## Data We Access
When authorized by the user, the app may access QuickBooks Online accounting data (e.g., Customers, Estimates) via Intuit OAuth, and may process purchase order documents received via email.

## How We Use Data
Data is used solely to:
- Create or update accounting records (e.g., Estimates) in QuickBooks Online
- Export PDFs for internal review
- Draft reply emails for human approval

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share user data with third parties. Data is used only for the benefit of the original QuickBooks Online company that authorized the app.
